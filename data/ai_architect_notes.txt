AI Architect Responsibilities:
The AI Architect is responsible for designing end-to-end AI systems. They must choose the right models, design RAG pipelines, and implement multi-agent orchestration. One of the most critical skills is creating effective retrieval strategies using chunking and overlap.

Importance of Chunk Overlap:
Chunk overlap is very important in RAG systems. Without proper overlap, important context can be lost at chunk boundaries. For example, if a sentence is split between two chunks, the model may not understand the full meaning. Using 50 to 100 characters of overlap significantly improves retrieval quality and reduces hallucinations.

Key Trends in 2026:
GraphRAG, Agentic workflows, SLMs, and proper evaluation frameworks are the biggest trends this year.
